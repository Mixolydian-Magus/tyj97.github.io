About

Tiedrich graduated from Stevens Institute of Technology in New York in 2014 with a degree in mechanical engineering. She currently resides in Wisconsin and works full-time designing spaceship parts.

In October 2016, Tiedrich married occasional co-writer and longtime boyfriend Norrin.

Tiedrich participates in Hourly Comic Day each year. Her two-panel hourly comics typically depict a day in her ordinary life.
