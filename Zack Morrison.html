Paranatural (genres: action, comedy, fantasy)

About

Morrison is 23 years old and lives in New York. He has been drawing Paranatural since high school.

Morrison suffers from chronic wrist inflammation that occasionally forces him to miss updates, though these occurances are much less frequent since he had restorative surgery.

According to Morrison, the town of Mayview is loosely based on his own hometown.

Morrison cites the following comics as influences on his own work:
