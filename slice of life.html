Slice of Life

Slice of Life is a webcomic genre, typically focusing on the everyday lives of the characters involved. Overarching plotlines are often minimal or absent entirely, but minor or short arcs occur frequently. Despite being more "ordinary" than other genres, there is still potential for overlap with genres such as Fantasy or Sci-Fi.
