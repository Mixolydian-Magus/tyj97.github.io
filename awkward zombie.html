Awkward Zombie

Awkward Zombie is a gag-a-day video game parody comic illustrated by Katie Tiedrich, with writing split between Tiedrich and her husband, Norrin. Each comic is a self-contained gag, typically poking fun at absurd mechanics or premises found in video games. The comic updates every Monday.

Recurring Games

The comic covers a wide variety of games; however, certain franchises receive more attention than others. Games and franchises which receive more coverage include Super Smash Bros., Fire Emblem, Ace Attorney, Metal Gear Solid, and Pokemon.

Hourly Comics Day

Each year, Tiedrich posts an Hourly Comics Day strip on the site. The strip is rarely video game related and instead tends to focus on Tiedrich's personal life.

History

The first page was posted on September 20, 2006 and has updated once weekly ever since. In 2015, it was cited by PC Gamer Magazine as one of the "best webcomics of 2015".
