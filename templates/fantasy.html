Fantasy

Fantasy is a genre of webcomic that typically refers to the setting - fantasy comics often occur in medieval or alternate reality settings, though this is not a requirement. Fantasy comics also tend to include mystical or magical elements - almost all involve some sort of magic system. Fantasy is very rarely the sole genre of a comic and is a very versatile genre, able to overlap with almost any other genre.
