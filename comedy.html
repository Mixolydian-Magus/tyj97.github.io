Comedy

Comedy is a genre of webcomic that makes humor the main focus. Each strip typically ends in a "punchline" designed to be comedic in some way. Comedy comics can be done in gag-a-day style or serialized. Very few comics are purely comedic; most comics exist somewhere on a spectrum of comedy to drama.
